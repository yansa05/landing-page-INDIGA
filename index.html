Indiga Studio

Indiga is an Indonesia Game Studio , independent game development and publishing studio. indiga was made in 2019, the first game released was jowo-the next leader but unfortunately the game failed or no one was downloaded. 2020 indiga tries to release a horror game, namely Hantu Survival.
